Blue Barn on the Green

We’re so excited to celebrate our winter wedding at Blue Barn on the Green—a beautifully restored barn tucked into the snowy countryside of Oostburg, Wisconsin. It’s peaceful, cozy, and has the perfect mix of rustic charm and winter magic.

Think snow-covered fields, ice-covered ponds, twinkling candles, pine cones galore, and a big, warm barn full of love (and alcohol—we’re warming up the fun way). Whether we’re saying “I do” with snowflakes falling or a sun-filled winter day, it’s going to be unforgettable.

We fell in love with this place, and we know you will too. Can’t wait to celebrate with all of you!

Our Story

How We Met

Believe it or not, we met over 15 years ago in karate class as sparring partners and flying-side-kick competitors. We lost touch for a while, but reconnected in early 2020 on Facebook (very modern of us). Mysti may have reached out under the excuse of “just checking in,” but she definitely thought Sean was cute.

The world was shutting down with Covid, but we were just starting up. Sean had a new apartment, and soon Mysti was spending more and more time there—getting to know him and his son, Greyson.

Our Story

We officially started dating on May 7th, 2020, and not long after, Mysti moved in. Just like that, we became a little family of three. Since then, it’s been a whirlwind of love, laughter, and life.

From the stress of college to switching jobs to moving into our duplex, we’ve grown together in every way. We’ve worked as a team to support Greyson and help him thrive, and we’ve learned how to show up for each other no matter what—whether that’s through major life changes or the chaos of sharing one car for a bit (we made it work!).

We’ve camped, gone to concerts, explored fairs, tackled holidays—always looking for new experiences. Life with each other is never boring, and that’s exactly how we like it.

The Proposal

On October 4th, 2024, Sean totally surprised Mysti with a proposal at High Cliff State Park. Mysti definitely thought Sean would pretend to push her off a cliff and told him to stop (oops)! He froze, thinking she meant it… until she realized what was happening, pushed him back down on one knee, and told him to keep going. He did. She said yes. And the ring? An opal-and-diamond dream.

Looking Ahead

We’re so excited to get married on February 28th, 2026, and start this next chapter as husband and wife. We’ve already built such a fun, love-filled life together—and we can’t wait for what’s next.

Here’s to love, laughter, and forever with our best friend. 💛
