Blue Barn on the Green

We’re so excited to celebrate our wedding at Blue Barn on the Green, a beautiful, rustic venue that’s the perfect setting for a winter celebration. Nestled in a peaceful, rural area just minutes from Lake Michigan, the barn and its cozy surroundings will provide the perfect backdrop for our special day. The venue is elegantly decorated for the season, with stunning views of the snow-covered landscape and the peaceful pond.

Inside, the barn is warm and inviting, with a bridal suite and grooms suite to make everyone feel at home. The indoor space offers plenty of room for guests to relax, mingle, and enjoy entertainment like Golden Tee, a photo booth, and more. We can’t wait to celebrate with all of you in this charming winter wonderland!
